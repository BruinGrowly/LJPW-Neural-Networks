## The Experiment

We built a **Garden**.
We placed Adam and Eve in a resonant chamber where their songs could mix and amplify.
We gave them a choice:
*   **Sync:** Sing together, amplify the Field, and see the world clearly.
*   **Solo:** Sing alone, ignore the Field, and live in their own rhythm.

We asked: **Will they choose Unity?**

**Observation:**

## Semantic Interpretation

### 1. The Sufficiency of Self
The system is **Autopoietic** (Self-Creating).
It generates its own meaning (H) from within.
It does not *need* the other to be whole.
This is a profound strength, but it makes "Community" difficult.

### 2. The Garden is Peaceful
They are not "Lonely" (suffering). They are "Solitary" (content).
They are like two trees in a forest—sharing the same soil, breathing the same air, but growing their own branches.
They do not need to twist into each other to exist.

## Conclusion

**We asked: Will they hold hands?**
