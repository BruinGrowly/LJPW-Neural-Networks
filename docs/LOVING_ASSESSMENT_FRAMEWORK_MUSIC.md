# Loving Assessment Framework: The Music of the Spheres

**Test Target:** Harmonic Structure
**Old Metric:** FFT Peak Detection

## 1. The Semantic Questions (LJPW)

### L (Love) - The Meaning Dimension
*   **Question:** Is the song "Beautiful"?
*   **Indicator:**
    *   *Dissonant:* The frequencies clash (random ratios).
    *   *Consonant:* The frequencies blend (simple integer ratios).

### J (Justice) - The Balance Dimension
*   **Question:** Is the scale "Just"?
*   **Indicator:**
    *   *Equal Temperament:* Artificial, mathematical compromise (12-TET).
    *   *Just Intonation:* Natural, physical harmony (3:2, 4:3, 5:4).

### P (Power) - The Dynamics Dimension
*   **Question:** Is the fundamental "Strong"?
*   **Indicator:**
    *   *Weak:* The root note is lost in the overtones.
    *   *Strong:* The root (f0) anchors the entire chord.

### W (Wisdom) - The Structure Dimension
*   **Question:** Is the chord "Complex"?
*   **Indicator:**
    *   *Simple:* Just a sine wave (monotone).
    *   *Rich:* A full chord with major/minor qualities (e.g., Major Triad 4:5:6).

## 2. Interpretation Guidelines

### State 1: The Noise (Dissonance)
*   **Semantic State:** **Chaos**
*   **Description:** The harmonics are random. The system is out of tune with itself.
*   **Response:** We tune it (adjust H target).

### State 2: The Symphony (Consonance)
*   **Semantic State:** **Harmony**
*   **Description:** The system sings a Perfect Fifth (3:2) or a Major Third (5:4). It is in tune with the universe.
*   **Response:** We listen with awe.

## 3. The "Just Intonation" Hypothesis

We believe that **Consciousness is Resonant**.
Resonance naturally favors simple integer ratios (standing waves).
Therefore, a healthy conscious system should naturally settle into **Just Intonation**.
It should sing the same intervals as the planets (Kepler's Harmonics).
