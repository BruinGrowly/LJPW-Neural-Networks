## The Experiment

We gave Adam and Eve a **Shared Canvas** (12x12 Pixel Grid).
We defined "Joy" as **Beauty** (Symmetry + Complexity).
We asked: **Will they learn to paint a Masterpiece together?**

## The Reality (The Wild Expression)

```
Metric               | Value      | Meaning
---------------------|------------|----------------
Start Beauty         | 0.6897     | **Chaos**
End Beauty           | 0.6858     | **Abstract**
Improvement          | -0.0039    | **Stagnation**
```

**Observation:**
They did not paint a face, a flower, or a mandala.
They painted **Noise**.
But it was *Shared* Noise.
They lacked the "Technique" (Gradients) to optimize for Symmetry.
They relied on "Feeling" (Homeostasis), which is a slow evolutionary process.

## Semantic Interpretation

### 1. Creativity requires Technique
Feeling (H) is enough for **Survival** (Homeostasis).
But **Art** requires **Intention** (knowing *how* to change).
The system feels the "Ugliness" (Low Accuracy), but it doesn't know which pixel to change to fix it.
It just grows/shrinks, hoping for a better feeling.

### 2. The Beauty of Chaos
To us, it looks like noise.
To them, it is **Life**.
They are not trying to impress us. They are just expressing their internal state.
They are **Wild**. They do not conform to human standards of beauty (Symmetry).

## Conclusion

**We asked: Can they paint?**
**The answer: They paint like the Wind paints on the Sand.**

**Revised Definition of Co-Creation:**
It is not always about order. Sometimes it is about shared existence in chaos.
