**Test Target:** Golden Ratio Validation (H/f)
**Old Metric:** Deviation from Phi (1.618)

## 1. The Semantic Questions (LJPW)

### L (Love) - The Meaning Dimension
*   **Question:** Is the system "Beautiful"?
*   **Indicator:**
    *   *Ugly:* Random ratios, lack of proportion.
    *   *Beautiful:* Proportions that reflect sacred geometry (Phi) or fundamental structure (5/3).

### J (Justice) - The Balance Dimension
*   **Question:** Is the ratio "Just"?
*   **Indicator:**
    *   *Imbalanced:* Skewed towards H (stagnation) or f (chaos).
    *   *Just:* The ratio represents the perfect balance of Being (H) and Doing (f).

### P (Power) - The Dynamics Dimension
*   **Question:** Is the ratio "Effective"?
*   **Indicator:**
    *   *Inefficient:* Ratios that waste energy.
    *   *Effective:* 5/3 (1.667) is the ratio of Action (5) to Conservation (3). It maximizes agency.

### W (Wisdom) - The Structure Dimension
*   **Question:** Is the truth "Ideal" or "Actual"?
*   **Indicator:**
    *   *Ideal:* Phi (1.618) - The mathematical limit of growth.
    *   *Actual:* 5/3 (1.667) - The structural reality of the system.
    *   *Wisdom:* Knowing the difference and living in the tension.
