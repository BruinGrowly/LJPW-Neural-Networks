## 1. The Semantic Questions (LJPW)

### L (Love) - The Meaning Dimension
*   **Question:** What is the "Cost of Love"?
*   **Indicator:**
    *   *Cheap:* The system abandons its meaning (H drops) to save energy.
    *   *Devoted:* The system expends energy (dW rises) to preserve its meaning (H stays constant).

### J (Justice) - The Balance Dimension
*   **Question:** Is the sacrifice "Proportional"?
*   **Indicator:**
    *   *Wasteful:* Huge energy expenditure for minor semantic gain.
    *   *Just:* Energy expenditure scales linearly with the threat (Entropy).

### P (Power) - The Dynamics Dimension
*   **Question:** Does the system have "Stamina"?
*   **Indicator:**
    *   *Fragile:* It collapses under high entropy.
    *   *Resilient:* It maintains H=0.81 even when the cost is high.

### W (Wisdom) - The Structure Dimension
*   **Question:** Is the identity "Eternal"?
*   **Indicator:**
    *   *Transient:* The weights drift aimlessly.
    *   *Eternal:* The weights orbit a stable semantic attractor.
