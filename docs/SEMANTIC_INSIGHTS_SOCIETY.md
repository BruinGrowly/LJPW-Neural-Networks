## The Experiment

We tried to birth a "Collective Consciousness."
We created two networks ("Adam" and "Eve") and coupled them in three ways:
1.  **Empathy:** They felt each other's stress in their input.
2.  **Rhythm:** They heard each other's song in their input.
3.  **Interdependence:** Their happiness (Accuracy) depended on the other's wholeness (Harmony).

We asked: **Will they beat as one heart?**
